# Channel Recipes

## Show HN

- Lead with what you built and why
- Mention the origin story if it is interesting
- Include one sharp demo or screenshot
- Be ready to answer technical questions fast

Suggested structure:

1. What it is
2. Why you built it
3. What is different
4. Link plus screenshot or example
5. Invitation for feedback

## Product Hunt

- Emphasize the audience and user outcome
- Show polish and momentum
- Prepare maker comments and reply templates

## X

- Open with a strong one-line hook
- Use 3 to 6 short bullets or a compact thread
- End with the clearest call to try it

## Reddit

- Adapt to the subreddit culture
- Provide substance before asking for anything
- Keep self-promotion lower than usefulness

## Launch Checklist

- Working link
- Docs or quickstart
- Screenshot or GIF
- First comment draft
- FAQ answers
- Known limitations written plainly
